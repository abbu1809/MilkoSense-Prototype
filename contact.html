Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about MilkoSense

What is MilkoSense?

MilkoSense is an AI-assisted milk quality testing system that uses IoT sensors and machine learning to detect adulteration and assess milk quality in real-time.

How accurate is the system?

Our system achieves 99% accuracy in milk quality classification using advanced Random Forest algorithms and multi-parameter sensor analysis.

What parameters does it measure?

MilkoSense measures pH, temperature, turbidity, TDS (Total Dissolved Solids), and gas concentrations using specialized sensors for comprehensive quality assessment.

Is this system available for commercial use?

Currently, MilkoSense is a college project prototype. We're exploring opportunities for commercialization and partnerships with dairy industry stakeholders.

Can I contribute to the project?

We welcome collaborations from researchers, developers, and organizations interested in food safety technology. Please reach out to discuss potential partnerships.
