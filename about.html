About MilkoSense

Revolutionary AI-powered milk quality testing system that combines advanced sensor technology with machine learning to ensure the highest standards of dairy safety and purity.

What is MilkoSense?

MilkoSense is an innovative IoT-based system designed to address the critical challenges of milk quality assessment in the dairy industry. By leveraging advanced sensor technology and artificial intelligence, our system provides accurate, real-time analysis of milk quality parameters.

The system integrates multiple sensors including pH, temperature, turbidity, TDS, and gas sensors to comprehensively evaluate milk quality. Machine learning algorithms analyze this data to detect adulteration, predict spoilage, and classify milk quality grades with remarkable accuracy.

Sensor Technology

Advanced multi-parameter sensor array providing comprehensive milk quality analysis with scientific precision.

pH Sensor

Measures acidity/alkalinity levels

Range: 6.0 - 7.0
Optimal: 6.4 - 6.8

Temperature Sensor

Monitors thermal conditions

Range: 0°C - 50°C
Optimal: 15°C - 20°C

Turbidity Sensor

Detects cloudiness/particles

Range: 0 - 100 NTU
Optimal: 10 - 20 NTU

TDS Sensor

Measures dissolved solids

Range: 0 - 2000 ppm
Optimal: 800 - 1200 ppm

Gas Sensor

Detects volatile compounds

Type: MQ135
Detects: NH₃, NOx, alcohol

Quality Standards

Industry-standard quality parameters and thresholds for comprehensive milk quality assessment.

A

Grade A (Excellent)

pH Level: 6.5 - 6.7
Temperature: 15°C - 20°C
Turbidity: 10 - 15 NTU
TDS: 800 - 1000 ppm

Premium quality milk suitable for direct consumption

B

Grade B (Good)

pH Level: 6.4 - 6.5
Temperature: 10°C - 25°C
Turbidity: 15 - 20 NTU
TDS: 700 - 1200 ppm

Good quality milk for processing

C

Grade C (Fair)

pH Level: 6.2 - 6.4
Temperature: 5°C - 30°C
Turbidity: 20 - 25 NTU
TDS: 600 - 1400 ppm

Requires treatment before use
