Quick Reference Guide

Industry standard values for milk quality assessment

Grade A (Excellent)

pH: 6.5 - 6.7
Temperature: 15°C - 20°C
Turbidity: 10 - 15 NTU
TDS: 800 - 1000 ppm

Grade B (Good)

pH: 6.4 - 6.5
Temperature: 10°C - 25°C
Turbidity: 15 - 20 NTU
TDS: 700 - 1200 ppm

Grade C (Fair)

pH: 6.2 - 6.4
Temperature: 5°C - 30°C
Turbidity: 20 - 25 NTU
TDS: 600 - 1400 ppm
